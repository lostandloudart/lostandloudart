We build immersive narratives for the curious.

Lost and Loud is an interdisciplinary studio crafting experiences where film, sculpture, code, and sound collide. We chase the imperfect, the textured, the generous chaos that makes an idea feel alive.

From remote Patagonia to digital spheres, we collaborate with artists, researchers, and dreamers to turn speculative concepts into tactile realities. Every project is a new language: we prototype, we experiment, we leave room for the uncanny to speak.

What we craft.

Cinematic Worlds

Stories told through moving light and crafted space. We create immersive short and long-form films, blending 3D animation, cinematography, and architectural composition to draw viewers into living narratives.

Digital Realities

From sculpted models to generative art, we explore the intersection of design and technology. Our work spans 3D sculpting, AI-assisted visuals, and bespoke motion pieces—each crafted as a singular visual experience.

Visual Storycraft

Posters, illustrations, and edited photography that extend cinematic language into still form. We transform frames into emotion, designing visuals that linger long after the screen fades.
